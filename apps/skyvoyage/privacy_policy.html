Sky Voyage — Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here is exactly what we collect and why.

Effective date: 28 March 2026  ·  Last updated: 28 March 2026

1. Introduction

Sky Voyage ("we", "our", or "us") is a mobile game published on Google Play for Android devices. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you play Sky Voyage (the "App").

By downloading or playing Sky Voyage you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please uninstall the App and do not use it.

This policy applies to all users worldwide, including users in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, California (CCPA), and other jurisdictions with applicable privacy laws.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect your real name, email address, phone number, location, camera, microphone, contacts, or any other sensitive personal data.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Operate and save your game progress locally on your device (SharedPreferences).
  • Sync your best distance score to the Google Play Games global leaderboard.
  • Unlock and display achievements via Google Play Games Services.
  • Deliver rewarded video ads (revive run, double coins, free power-up) and interstitial ads via Google AdMob.
  • Process in-app purchases (gem packs, cosmetics, No Ads) via Google Play Billing.
  • Collect anonymised analytics to understand how players experience the game (Firebase Analytics).
  • Monitor app stability and fix crashes (Firebase Crashlytics).
  • Remote-tune difficulty curves without an app update (Firebase Remote Config).

4. Advertising & Ad Networks

Sky Voyage shows ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your Android Advertising ID to serve personalised ads based on your interests. You can opt out of interest-based advertising at any time:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID (Android 12+) or Opt out of Ads Personalisation (Android < 12).
  • Alternatively, tap "No Ads" in the in-game shop to remove all ads entirely.

AdMob's own privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

6. Data Storage & Retention

On-device: All game progress (profile, coins, gems, stars, equipped items) is stored locally in Android SharedPreferences. No account or log-in is required. Uninstalling the App deletes all local data.

Google Play Games cloud save (when enabled): Your best distance, level, and achievements are synced to your Google account. You can delete this data via your Google account settings at myaccount.google.com.

Firebase Analytics / Crashlytics: Anonymised event data is retained for up to 14 months by default (configurable in the Firebase console). Crash reports are retained for 90 days.

We do not maintain our own servers or databases that store personal information.

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA / GDPR-K)

Sky Voyage is rated Everyone (E) / PEGI 3 and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA).

We do not use behavioural advertising for users identified as children. If you believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us at the address below and we will delete it promptly.

Ads shown to child-directed audiences are configured as non-personalised in compliance with Google's Families Policy.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access & portability — request a copy of data we hold about you.
  • Deletion — request deletion of your data. For local data, simply uninstall the App. For Play Games cloud data, use your Google account settings.
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Opt-out of personalised ads — reset or delete your Android Advertising ID, or purchase the No Ads option in-game.
  • Opt-out of analytics — disable Firebase Analytics data collection by toggling "Analytics" off in Settings → Privacy inside the App (coming in a future update), or by resetting your Advertising ID.

EEA/UK users have additional rights under the GDPR / UK GDPR: the right to object, restrict processing, and lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

California residents have rights under the CCPA/CPRA, including the right to know, delete, and opt out of sale. We do not sell personal information.

9. Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect the information we handle:

  • All data transmitted to Firebase and Google services uses TLS encryption in transit.
  • Release builds are signed with a private keystore that is not stored in source control.
  • No personally identifiable information is logged in production builds.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but we take it seriously.

Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
